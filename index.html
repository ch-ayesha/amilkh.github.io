About Me

As the founder of Virufy, I believe in the power of technology to transform lives. Our mission is to bridge the gap between innovation and humanity by empowering underserved populations with opportunities that were once beyond their reach.

Through our AI-powered cough analysis technology, we've helped thousands of people access early disease detection, competed in international competitions, and achieved remarkable milestones in digital health innovation.

At Virufy, we are not just teaching code; we are shaping the future of healthcare technology and social impact.
